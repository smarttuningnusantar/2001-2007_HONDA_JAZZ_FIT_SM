Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0351, P0352, P0353, P0354, P0355, P0356, P0357 and/or P0358 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ignition coil and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
