ATF Feed Pipe Replacement - Flywheel Housing
  1. Remove the snap ring (A), then remove the ATF feed pipe (B) and feed pipe flange (C).
  1. Replace the ATF feed pipe, and install the new ATF feed pipe by aligning its tab (D) with the guide on the housing.
  1. Install the new O-ring (E) over the feed pipe, and install the new O-ring (F) in the feed pipe flange, then install the feed flange over the feed pipe.
  1. Secure the feed pipe in the housing with the snap ring.
