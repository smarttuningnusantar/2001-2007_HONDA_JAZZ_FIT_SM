Fuel Fill Door Opener Replacement
  1. Remove the screws (A) securing the opener cover (B), then remove the opener cover.
  1. Remove the bolt (C), then remove the fuel fill door opener handle (D).
  1. Disconnect the fuel fill door cable (E) from the fuel fill door release handle. Take care not to bend the cable.
  1. Install the fuel fill door release handle in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the fuel fill door opener cable is connected properly.
    • Make sure the fuel fill door opens properly.
