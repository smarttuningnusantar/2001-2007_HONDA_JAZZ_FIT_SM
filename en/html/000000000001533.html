DTC Troubleshooting: P2118 (40)
DTC P2118:
 Throttle Actuator Current Range/Perfomance Problem
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Do the DTC CLEAR in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC P0638 and/or P2118 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the throttle body and at the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 10 seconds.
  1. Connect the ECM test harness and the test pin box to the wire harness only, not to the ECM.
  1. Check for continuity between test pin box terminals No. 122 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Disconnect the throttle body 6P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between test pin box terminal No. 122 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM connector No. 5 terminal No. 1 (test pin box terminal No. 122) and the throttle body, then go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Check for continuity between test pin box terminal No. 123 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM connector No. 5 terminal No. 2 (test pin box terminal No. 123) and the throttle body, then go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. At the throttle body side, measure resistance between the throttle body 6P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 6.
Is there about 1.5 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 23 .
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Disconnect the throttle body 6P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between test pin box terminals No. 122 and No. 123.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM connector No. 5 terminal No. 1 (test pin box terminal No. 122) and the ECM connector No. 5 terminal No. 2 (test pin box terminal No. 123), then go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. At the throttle body side, check for continuity between the throttle body 6P connentor terminals No. 2 and No. 3.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Go to Step 15 .
  1. At the throttle body side, measure resistance between the throttle body 6P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 6.
Is there about 1.5 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 23 .
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the throttle body.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Do the ECM RESET in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS learn precedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P2118 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the throttle actuator and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P2118 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the throttle actuator and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Replace the original ECM.
