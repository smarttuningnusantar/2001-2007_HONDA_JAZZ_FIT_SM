  1. Check for continuity between gauge assembly connector terminal A17 and body ground while you move the shift lever in and out of [P] position.
Is there continuity with the shift lever in [P] position, and no continuity with the shift lever out of [P]?
YES -
Faulty key interlock system circuit in the gauge assembly, replace the gauge assembly.■
