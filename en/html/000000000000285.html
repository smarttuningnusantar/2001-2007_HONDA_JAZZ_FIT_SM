Symptom Troubleshooting: Audio driving instructions cannot be heard
Audio driving instructions cannot be heard
  1. Check the audio driving instructions volume setting.
Is it set to OFF?
YES -
Set the volume to an audible lebel.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Perform the System Link check under the picture diagnosis.
Can the sound be heard?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Check for open and short to body ground in the speaker circuits.
Are the circuits OK?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Repair open or short to body ground in the wire speaker circuits.■
