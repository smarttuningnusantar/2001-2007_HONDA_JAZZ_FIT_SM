Immobilizer Control Unit-Receiver Replacement
  1. Remove the dashboard lower cover.
  1. Remove the steering column covers.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) from the immobilizer control unit-receiver (B).
  1. Remove the two screws and the immobilizer control unit-receiver from the ignition key cylinder (C).
  1. Install the immobilizer control unit-receiver in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After replacement, register the immobilizer control unit-receiver with PGM-Tester, then check the immobilizer system.
