Shim Selection Formula:
  1. Select the proper 72 mm shim from the chart. Follow the example below, and use the measurements you made in steps 4 and 5:

    (Basic Formula)

    (1) + (2) − (0.75 + 0.11) = shim thickness (maximum)
    (1) + (2) − (0.75 + 0.18) = shim thickness (minimum)

    • Add distance (1) (step 4) to distance (2) (step 5).
    • 0.75 mm (0.029 in): Spring washer, a dimension in the installation.
    • 0.11 mm (0.004 in): Minimum thrust clearance.
    • 0.18 mm (0.007 in): Maximum thrust clearance.

    (For example)

    2.41 + 0.22 − (0.75 + 0.11) = 1.77 mm (0.070 in.)
    2.41 + 0.22 − (0.75 + 0.18) = 1.70 mm (0.067 in.)

  1. Take the middle value of the minimum value and the maximum value, and select shim of 1.74 mm (0.069 in).
