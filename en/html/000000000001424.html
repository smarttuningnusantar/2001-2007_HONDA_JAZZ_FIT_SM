Child Seat Anchor Fitting Replacement
Standard Attachment Point
  1. Remove the child seat anchor fitting cover (A) from the middle attachment point (B) on the rear of the headliner.
  1. Remove the child seat anchor fitting (C). Do not remove the toothed washer (D) from the anchor fitting.
  1. Install the child seat anchor fitting in the reverse order of removal.
Other Attachment Points
Remove the child seat anchor fitting from the middle attachment point, and reinstall the anchor fitting to other attachment point.
Installation to right attachment point:
  1. Remove the anchor cap (A) from the right attachment point (B).
  1. Reinstall the child seat anchor fitting (C).
  1. Reinstall the child seat anchor fitting cover (D).
  1. Install the anchor cap (A) to the middle attachment point (E).
Installation to left attachment point:
  1. Pry the lid (A) of the rear center shoulder seat belt retractor cover (B) down, and remove the bolt (C) from the left attachment point (D). Do not remove the toothed washer (E) from the bolt.
  1. Reinstall the child seat anchor fitting (E).
  1. Store the removed retractor bolt (C) and the child seat anchor fitting cover (F).
