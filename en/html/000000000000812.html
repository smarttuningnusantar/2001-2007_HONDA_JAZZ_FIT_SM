7-speed Mode Main Switch/Steering Shift Switch Test
SRS components are located in this area. Review the following articles in the SRS section before performing repairs or service;
  • Component Locations.
  • Precautions and Procedures.
  1. Remove the steering column covers upper and lower.
  1. Remove the driver's airbag from the steering wheel.
  1. Disconnect the main switch (7SPEED MODE)/steering shift switch connector (A).
  1. Check the main switch (7SPEED MODE) for continuity between connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2. There should be toggled continuity and no continuity between terminals every time the main switch (7SPEED MODE) (B) pushes.
  1. Check the steering shift switch plus (+) for continuity between connector terminals No. 4 and No. 2. There should be continuity while pressing both plus (+) of the steering shift switches, and no continuity when the plus (+) switch released.
  1. Check the steering shift switch minus (−) for continuity between connector terminals No. 3 and No. 2. There should be continuity while pressing both minus (−) of the steering shift switches, and no continuity when the minus (−) switch released.
  1. If either test is faulty, replace the main switch (7SPEED MODE)/steering shift switch. If the switch is OK, repair the wire harnesses between the cable reel and the PCM.
