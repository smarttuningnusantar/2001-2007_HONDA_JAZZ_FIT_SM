Coolant Temperature Indicator/Warning Light Troubleshooting
Before testing, check the No. 8 (15A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box and No. 16 (7.5A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Start the engine and check the Malfunction Indicator Lamp (MIL).
Does the MIL come on?
YES -
Troubleshoot the DTC , and recheck.
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Disconnect the ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between the ECM/PCM connector E (31P) No. 24 terminal and gauge assembly connector C (30P) No. 11 terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the gauge assembly and recheck.■
NO -
Check the open in the wire and the connection condition of the terminal.■
