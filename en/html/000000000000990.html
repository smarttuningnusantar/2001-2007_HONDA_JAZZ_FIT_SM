Resetting the Power Window Control Unit - With UP-AUTO Function
Resetting the power window control unit is required after performing the following procedures:
  • Shutdown of the battery power
  • Shutdown of the electric power caused by both No. 12 (10A) and No. 29 (20A) fuses in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  • Open circuit caused by disconnected 20P connector from the power window master switch
  • Removal of the regulator, glass or glass run channel
  1. Make sure the glass is installed properly.
  1. Close the driver's door.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the No. 29 (20A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. After 1 second, turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. After 5 seconds, install the No. 29 (20A) fuse to the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Make sure the driver's window does not work in AUTO with the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Turn the ignition switch START (III) and start the engine.
  1. Move the driver's window all the way down by manual DOWN of the driver's power window switch.
  1. Open the driver's door.
  1. Move the driver's window all the way up by manual UP of the driver's power window switch and hold on for 1 second.
  1. If the window does not work in AUTO, reset the power window master switch according to the above procedures again.
