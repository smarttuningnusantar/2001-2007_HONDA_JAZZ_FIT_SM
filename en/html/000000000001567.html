Fuel Tank Replacement
  1. Drain the fuel tank.
  1. Jack up the vehicle, and support it with jackstands.
  1. Disconnect the hoses (A). Slide back the clamps, then twist the hoses as you pull to avoid damaging them.
  1. Remove the fuel tank guard (B) and the center front floor cross beam (C).
  1. Place a jack, or other support, under the tank.
  1. Remove the strap bolts, and let the straps (D) fall free.
  1. Remove the fuel tank (E). If it sticks to the undercoat on its mount, carefully pry it off the mount.
  1. Install the remaining parts in the reverse order of removal.
