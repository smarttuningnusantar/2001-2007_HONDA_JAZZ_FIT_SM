Side Sill Panel Replacement
  1. Remove the side sill panel.
    -1.
    Remove the front clip (A) and expansion clips (B).
    -2.
    Slide the side sill panel (C) forward, and remove it. The side clips (D) will stay in the body.
    -3.
    Remove the side clips from the body.
  1. Replace any damaged clips.
  1. Install the side clips on the side sill panel.
  1. Hold the panel up, and fit all the side clips into the holes in the body, then push on the panel until the clips snap into place.
  1. Install all the expansion clips and front clip.
