Air Mix Control Cable Adjustment
NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is symmetrical.
  1. From under the dash, open the clamp (A), then disconnect the air mix control cable (B) from the air mix control linkage (C).
  1. Set the temperature control dial to Max Cool.
  1. Turn the air mix control linkage fully clockwise as shown above, and hold it. Attach the air mix control cable to the air mix control linkage, then install the air mix control cable housing into the clamp.
