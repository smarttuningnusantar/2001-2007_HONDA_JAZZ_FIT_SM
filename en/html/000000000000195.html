Rear Fender Cover Replacement - Type B
For Some Models
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the rear fender cover and body.
  1. Remove the rear damper unit, refer to the 2002 Jazz/Fit Shop Manual, P/N 62SAA00.
  1. Remove the rear fender cover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the screws (B) securing the rear bumper (C) and rear fender cover.
    -2.
    Remove the bolts (D), and clips (E, F), then remove the rear fender cover.
  1. Install the rear fender cover in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
