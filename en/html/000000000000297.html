Clock Display Size and Location Adjustment
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. When the disclaimer is shown on the navigation display unit, select ‘‘OK.''
  1. When the ‘‘Enter destination by: '' screen is shown, select ‘‘Setup.''
  1. When the ‘‘Setup Screen'' is shown, select Display ‘‘Off.''
  1. Select the numbers of the clock to change the clock size and location.
    • If the clock is small and in the lower right hand corner of the screen, it will increase in size and move to the center of the screen.
    • If the clock is large and in the center of the screen, it will decrease in size and move to the lower right hand corner of the screen.
  1. Push the MENU button to return to the ‘‘Enter destination by:'' screen. The clock display settings will be saved.
