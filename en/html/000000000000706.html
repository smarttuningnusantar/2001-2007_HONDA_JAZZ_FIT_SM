DTC Troubleshooting: P0141 (65-2)
DTC P0141 (65-2):
 Secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) Heater Circuit Malfunction
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM.
  1. Start the engine.
Is DTC P0141 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose wires at the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and at the ECM / PCM .■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 4P connector.
  1. At the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) side, measure resistance between the HO2S 4P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 4.
Is there about 11 Ω (at room temperture)?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Replace the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) .■
  1. Check continuity between body ground and the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 4P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 4 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) .■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the secondary HO2S 4P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 4.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal E6 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM/PCM (E6) and the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) .■
NO -
Substitute a known-good ECM/PCM and recheck . If symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
  1. Measure voltage between the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 4P connector terminal No. 4 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Check the No. 16 ACG (7.5A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box. If the fuse is OK, repair open in the wire between the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and No. 16 ACG (7.5A) fuse.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Reconnect the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 4P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between ECM/PCM connector terminal E6 and A5.
Is there 0.1 V or less?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (E6) and the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) .■
NO -
Substitute a known-good ECM/PCM and recheck . If symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
