DTC Troubleshooting: 52
DTC 52:
 Motor Stuck OFF
  1. Check the No. 4 (30 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Reinstall the fuse, and go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck. If fuse is blown, go to Step 4 .
  1. Disconnect the VSA control unit 46P connector.
  1. Mesure the voltage between VSA control unit 46P connector terminal No. 45 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between No. 4 (30 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box and VSA modulator-control unit.■
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle at 15 km/h (9 mph) or more.
  1. Check the DTCs using the HDS.
Does the ABS indicator come on, and are DTC 52 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections in the VSA control unit 46P connector. If connections are good, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
