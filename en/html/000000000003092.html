DTC Troubleshooting: 9
DTC 9:
 A Problem in the Air Mix Control Linkage, Door, or Motor
  1. Test the air mix control motor.
Is the air mix control motor OK?
YES -
Substitute a known-good climate control unit , and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original climate control unit .■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Remove the air mix control motor.
  1. Check the air mix control linkage and door for smooth movement.
Do the air mix control linkage and door move smoothly?
YES -
Replace the air mix control motor.■
NO -
Repair the air mix control linkage or door.■
