The SRS Indicator Stays On When In ‘‘SCS'' Menu Method
NOTE:
  • Inability to retrieve DTCs with the HDS. Retrieve the flash codes with the SCS mode.
  • A new SRS unit must sense the entire system is OK before completing its initial self-test. The most common cause of an incomplete self-test is the failure to replace all deployed parts after a collision, in particular, seat belt tensioners and seat belt buckle tensioners.
  • An incomplete self-test prevents the HDS from retrieving DTCs, although flash codes are available in the SCS mode.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
Does the SRS indicator go off when the DTC memory is erased?
