DTC Troubleshooting: P0811 (43-2)
DTC P0811:
 Problem in Start Clutch Control System
For KB, KE, KG, and KQ Models
NOTE:
  • Record all freeze data and review General Troubleshooting Information before you troubleshoot.
  • The illustration shows the example of connector terminal arrangement; connector terminal arrangement varies with the models.
  1. Check whether the another DTC is indicated.
Is the another DTC indicated?
YES -
Perform the troubleshooting for the indicated DTC(s), and recheck for DTC P0811 after troubleshooting.
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect CVT start clutch pressure control valve connector from the control valve body.
  1. Start the engine, and shift to the D position.
  1. Check if the vehicle moves.
Does the vehicle move?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Replace the start clutch.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Connect CVT start clutch pressure control valve connector.
  1. Start the engine, and shift to the D position.
  1. Check if the vehicle creeps and check the creeping speed on level ground.
Does the vehicle creep and is the creeping speed about 5 km/h (3 mph)?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Replace the control valve body. ■
  1. Measure the stall speed RPM in the D, S, L, and R positions.
Is the stall speed over 3,500 rpm (min −1 )?
YES -
Replace the start clutch.■
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect CVT start clutch pressure control valve connector from the control valve body.
  1. Start the engine, and shift to the D position.
  1. Check if the vehicle moves.
Does the vehicle move?
YES -
The system is OK, update the PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good PCM, replace the original PCM .■
NO -
Replace the start clutch.■
