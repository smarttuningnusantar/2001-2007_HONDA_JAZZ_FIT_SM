Front Door Outer Seal Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the door.
  • Use a clip remover to remove the clips.
  1. At the front pillar, remove the door stop mounting bolt (A).
  1. Detach the clips (B, C, D), then remove the door outer seal (E).
  1. Install the seal in the reverse order of removal and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Make sure the outer seal is installed in the holder (F) securely.
    • Apply liquid thread lock to door stop mounting bolt before installation.
    • Check for water leaks.
