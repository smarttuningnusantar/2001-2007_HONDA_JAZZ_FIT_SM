DTC Troubleshooting: 1-3
DTC 1-3:
 Short to Another Wire or Decreased Resistance in Driver's Airbag Inflator
Special Tools Required

SRS inflator simulator
 07SAZ-TB4011A

SRS simulator lead C
 07TAZ-SZ5011A
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the D1o connector from the D1i connector (A).
  1. Connect the special tool (2 Ω connector) to the D1i connector (A).
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 1-3 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Short in the driver's airbag; replace the driver's airbag. ■
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the U1o connector (A) from the SRS unit.
  1. Disconnect the SRS inflator simulator (special tool) from the SRS simulator lead C.
  1. Check resistance between the A and B terminals of the SRS simulator lead C (special tool). There should be 1 MΩ or more.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Go to Step 13 .
NO -
Replace the cable reel. ■
  1. Install the special tool (070AZ-SAA0100) to No. 3 and No. 4 terminals of the U1o connector.
  1. Check resistance between the A and B terminals of the SRS simulator lead C (special tool). There should be 1 MΩ or more.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Replace the the SRS unit. ■
NO -
