Door Lock Knob Switch Test - With Super Locking
With Super Locking (Driver's door):
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 6P connector (A) from the super locking actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 3 and No. 4 terminals when the door lock knob switch is LOCK position.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 1 and No. 4 terminals when the door lock knob switch is UNLOCK position.
With Super Locking (Passenger's door):
  1. Remove the passenger's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 6P connector (A) from the super locking actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 1 and No. 4 terminals when the door lock knob switch is UNLOCK position.
