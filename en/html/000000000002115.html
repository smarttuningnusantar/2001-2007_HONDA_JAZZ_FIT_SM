DTC Troubleshooting: 75
DTC 75:
 Lateral Acceleration Sensor (Joint Failure)
  1. Check the installation condition of the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor.
Is it correctly installed?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
If the loose the mounting bolts, tighten the mounting bolts properly, and recheck.■
  1. Park the vehicle on a level surface. Check YAW RATES LATERAL in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is the result?
YES -
Check for loose connector terminals in the VSA control unit 46P connector and repair if necessary. If terminals are OK, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
Replace the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor. ■
