7-speed Mode Steering Shift - (Minus) Switch Circuit Troubleshooting
For KB, KE, KG, and KQ (L15A1 engine) Models (6-position CVT+7Speed Mode)
SRS components are located in this area. Review the following articles in the SRS before performing repairs or service;
  • Component Locations.
  • Precautions and Procedures.
  1. Remove the steering wheel.
  1. Check for continuity between cable reel subharness 13P connector terminals No. 7 and No. 9 while pressing steering shift − (minus) switch and when the switch is released.
Is there continuity while pressing steering shift − (minus) switch and no continuity when the switch released?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Disconnect steering shift − (minus) switch connector.
  1. Check for continuity between steering shift − (minus) switch connector terminals while pressing the switch and when the switch is released.
Is there continuity while pressing the switch and no continuity when the switch released?
YES -
Replace the cable reel subharness. ■
NO -
Replace steering shift − (minus) switch. ■
  1. Remove the cable reel.
  1. Check for continuity cable reel 13P connector terminal No. 9 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between cable reel 13P connector terminal No. 9 and ground (G402), or repair poor ground (G402).■
  1. Check for continuity between cable reel 13P connector terminal No. 7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to ground in the wire between cable reel 13P connector terminal No. 7 and PCM connector terminal B19.■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and shift to the D or S position.
  1. Measure the voltage between cable reel 13P connector terminals No. 7 and No. 9.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Replace the cable reel. ■
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Measure the voltage between PCM connector terminal B19 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between PCM connector terminal B19 and cable reel 13P connector.■
NO -
Check for loose or poor connections at PCM connector terminal B19. If the connection is OK, substitute a known-good PCM and recheck.■
