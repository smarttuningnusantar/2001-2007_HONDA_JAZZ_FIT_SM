CVT Driven Pulley Shaft Bearing Removal/Installation
Special Tools Required

Snap ring pliers
 07LGC-0010100

Handle driver
 07749-0010000

Oil seal driver attachment, 64 x 72 mm
  07JAD-PN00100
  1. Expand the snap ring of the driven pulley shaft bearing with the snap ring pliers, then remove the driven pulley shaft bearing with the special tools and a press.
  1. Expand the snap ring of the driven pulley shaft bearing with the snap ring pliers, then install the bearing part-way into the housing.
  1. Release the pliers, then push the bearing down into the housing until the snap ring snaps in place around it.
  1. After installing the bearing, verify that the snap ring are seated in the bearing and housing grooves, and that the ring and gap is 0-9 mm (0-0.35 in.)
