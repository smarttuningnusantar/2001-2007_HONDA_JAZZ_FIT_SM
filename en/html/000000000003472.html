Subfuel Pump Replacement
Special Tools Required

Fuel sender wrench, clysler fuel pump module wrench
No. 7998

, or equivalent, commercially available
  1. Remove the subfuel tank.
  1. Set the fuel subtank (A) to the vice.
  1. Using the tool, loosen the locknut (B). Remove the subfuel pump (C).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with a new gasket (D) and locknut plate (E).
    NOTE:
    • Align the marks (F) on the fuel tank and the fuel tank unit, then tighten a new locknut by hand.
    • After installation and tightening, check the base gasket visually or by hand to be sure the gasket is not pinched.
    • Do not coat the base gasket with engine oil.
