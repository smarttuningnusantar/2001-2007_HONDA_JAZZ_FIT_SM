HID Unit Replacement
  1. Turn the combination light switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the headlight assembly.
  1. Remove the bulb socket from the HID bulb.
  1. Remove the three mounting screws from the headlight housing (A).
  1. Disconnect the connector (B) and remove the HID unit.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
