ECM Replacement
NOTE: If the troubleshooting leads to replace the ECM after substituting a known-good ECM, reinstall the original ECM first, then do this procedure.
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (DLC) (A) located under the driver's side of the dashboard.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Make sure the HDS communicates with the ECM. If it doesn't, go to the DLC circuit troubleshooting.
  1. Select the READ DATA in the REPLACE ECM MENU with the HDS.
    NOTE: If the READ DATA fails, ask the customer about the fuel grade (alcohol contained level) in the fuel tank (main), and go to Step 5 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 10 seconds.
  1. Remove the glove box.
  1. Remove the passenger's dashboard under cover (A), side kick panel (B) and glove box inner case.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (C), and remove the nuts, then remove the ECM (D).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Reconnect the negative cable to battery.
  1. Select the PGM-FI system with the HDS.
  1. Select WRITE DATA in the REPLACE ECM MENU with the HDS.
    NOTE: If WRITE DATA fails, go to Step 13 .
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
    • If the engine starts, go to Step 26 .
    • If the engine does not start, go to Step 15 .
  1. Select MAIN FUEL TANK ALCOHOL CONTENT in the ADJUSTMENT MENU with the HDS.
  1. Select the appropriate ALCOHOL CONTENT in the HDS screen by the customer's information on Step 4 .
    NOTE: If the customer did not remember about it, go to Step 18 .
  1. Try to start the engine.
    • If the engine starts, go to Step 26 .
    • If the engine does not start, go to Step 18 .
  1. Select LOW ALCOHOL CONTENT and try to start the engine.
    • If the engine starts, go to Step 26 .
    • If the engine does not start, go to Step 19 .
  1. Select 50 % ALCOHOL CONTENT and try to start the engine.
    • If the engine starts, go to Step 26 .
    • If the engine does not start, go to Step 20 .
  1. Select 90 % ALCOHOL CONTENT and try to start the engine.
    • If the engine starts, go to Step 26 .
    • If the engine does not start, go to Step 21 .
  1. Select 100 % ALCOHOL CONTENT and try to start the engine.
    • If the engine starts, go to Step 26 .
    • If the engine does not start, go to Step 22 .
  1. Drain the fuel tank.
  1. Relieve the fuel pressure.
  1. Fill the fuel tank (main) with a gasoline.
  1. Select LOW ALCOHOL CONTENT and try to start the engine.
  1. Do the ETCS learn procedure.
