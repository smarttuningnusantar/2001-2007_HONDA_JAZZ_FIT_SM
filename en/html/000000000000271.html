Sunroof Switch Test/Replacement
  1. Remove the spotlight.
  1. Push the sunroof switch (A) out from behind the headliner.
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector (B) from the sunroof switch, and remove the sunroof switch.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the table.
  1. If the continuity is as not specified, replace the switch.
