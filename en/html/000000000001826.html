DTC Troubleshooting: P2228 (13-5), P2229 (13-6)
DTC P2228 (13-5):
 BARO Sensor Circuit Low Voltage
DTC P2229 (13-6):
 BARO Sensor Circuit High Voltage
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Is DTC P2228 or P2229 indicated?
YES -
Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time.■
