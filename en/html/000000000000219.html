Center Console Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • When prying with a flat-tip screwdriver, wrap it with protective tape to prevent damage.
  • Take care not to scratch the front seat, dashboard, and related parts.
  1. Remove the parking brake cover lid (A).
  1. M/T: Remove the shift knob.
  1. Remove the center console (A):
    -1.
    Remove the screws (B), and clips (C).
    -2.
    Lift the rear of the console up to detach the clip (D), and pull the console rearward to release the front hook (E) from the heater unit.
    -3.
    Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector (F).
  1. Install the console in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Make sure the wire harness is not pinched.
    • Set the rib (G) under the pocket onto the parking brake lever bracket (H) correctly.
