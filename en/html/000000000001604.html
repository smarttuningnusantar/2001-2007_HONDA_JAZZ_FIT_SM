DTC Troubleshooting: 17, 18
DTC 17:
 Torque Sensor Vcc1
DTC 18:
 Torque Sensor Vcc2
  1. Clear the DTC.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Wait at least 10 seconds.
Does the EPS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Stop the engine, and verify the DTC.
Is DTC 17 or DTC 18 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Do the troubleshooting for the DTC indicated.■
  1. Make sure the ignition switch is OFF, then disconnect EPS control unit connector B (14P) and the torque sensor 7P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector B (14P) terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the torque sensor and EPS control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector B (14P) terminal No. 1 and the torque sensor 7P connector terminal No. 2 (RHD: No. 6).
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the torque sensor and EPS control unit.■
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector B (14P) terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the torque sensor and EPS control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector B (14P) terminal No. 2 and the torque sensor 7P connector terminal No. 6 (RHD: No. 2).
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the torque sensor and EPS control unit.■
  1. Substitute a known-good EPS control unit, and connect all the disconnected connectors.
  1. Start the engine.
Does the EPS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Check for loose EPS control unit connectors. If necessary, replace the EPS control unit and recheck.■
  1. Stop the engine, and verify the DTC.
Is DTC 17 or DTC 18 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose torque sensor connectors.If necessary, substitute a known-good steering gearbox and recheck.■
NO -
Perform the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
