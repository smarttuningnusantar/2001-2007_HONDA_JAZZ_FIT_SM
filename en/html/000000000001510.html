  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the injector 2P connector from the problem cylinder.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the injector 2P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the injector and the PGM-FI main relay, then go to Step 15 .
  1. At the injector side, measure the resistance between the injector 2P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2.

YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Go to Step 13 .

  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the problem injector.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Do the ECM RESET in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS learn procedure.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0201, P0202, P0203 or P0204 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the injector and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
