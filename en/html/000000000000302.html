Front Seat Cushion Removal/Installation
  1. Remove the front seat.
  1. Remove the recline cover and center cover, with manual height, without manual height.
  1. With side airbag: From under the seat cushion, detach the side airbag connector clip. Release the hook springs and harness band.
  1. Release the hooks (A) from both sides the seat cushion.
  1. Release the seat-back cover (A) from the cushion frame spring (B), and release the hooks (C) from under the seat cushion.
  1. Remove the seat cushion cover/pad from the seat cushion frame.
  1. Install the cushion in the reverse order of removal. To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hooks.
