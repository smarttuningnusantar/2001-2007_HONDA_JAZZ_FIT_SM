Ignition Coil Removal/Installation
  1. When removing the front ignition coils, remove the intake manifold cover.
  1. When removing the rear ignition coils, remove the harness holder from the brackets.
  1. Disconnect the ignition coil connector, then remove the front ignition coils (A) and rear ignition coils (B).
  1. Install the ignition coils in the reverse order of removal.
