DTC Troubleshooting: 15
DTC 15:
 Ignition Coil Circuit Malfunction
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM.
  1. Start the engine, and check the DTC.
Is DTC is indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose wires at the ignition coils and at the ECM / PCM .■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Prepare a spare spark plug. Remove an ignition coil from the engine and attach the spare spark plug to the removed ignition coil.
  1. Connect the 3P connector to the ignition coil again. Start the engine with the spark plug connected to the ground, and check whether the sparks across the electrodes.
  1. Perform the same test on all of the other ignition coils. Check whether the sparks across the electrodes on all of the other ignition coils.
Do all ignition coils spark?
YES -
Substitute a known-good ECM/PCM and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Exchange the ignition coil of the cylinder that does not spark with an ignition coil of the normal cylinder. perform the same test and check whether the sparks.
Does it spark?
YES -
Replace the ignition coil that does not spark.■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the ignition coil 3P connector from the problem cylinder.
  1. Check for continuity between the ignition coil 3P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ignition coil and G101.■
