DTC Troubleshooting: P1107 (13-1), P1108 (13-2)
DTC P1107 (13-1):
 BARO Sensor Circuit Low Voltage
DTC P1108 (13-2):
 BARO Sensor Circuit High Voltage
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Is DTC P1107 or P1108 indicated?
YES -
Substitute a known-good ECM/PCM and recheck. If symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time.■
