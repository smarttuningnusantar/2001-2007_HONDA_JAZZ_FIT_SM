DTC Troubleshooting: 21-01, 21-02, 22-01
DTC 21-01:
 Excessive Change of the Vehicle Speed Sensor Signal
DTC 21-02:
 Average for the Vehicle Speed and Engine Speed
DTC 22-01:
 Engine Speed Signal Circuit
NOTE:
  • The EPS indicator might light when the PGM-FI diagnosis reproduction is tested.
  • This is due to the judgment condition of DTC 21-01 or DTC 21-02, and the EPS system might be normal.
  • The condition that EPS indicator turn on; when the engine rotational speed is 2500 rpm (after the machine) or 4000 rpm or more (5 minutes in time of machine), velocity 0 km/h of the car is detected 20 seconds more continuously, in addition, it continued by multiplication for 10 seconds.
  • When EPS indicator lights in the PGM-FI diagnosis test, it is necessary to delete DTC because EPS system is normal.
  • When EPS system starts, if it continues the state of signal input of the velocity 10 km/h or more for 3 seconds on the condition of the engine speed 2800 rpm or less, EPS indicator keeps lighting on the other hand though EPS system begins assisting.

  1. Start the engine and check the tachometer.
