Steering Hanger Beam Replacement
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the dashboard.
  1. Remove these items from the dashboard:
    • Instrument visor
    • Gauge assembly
    • Inner glove box
    • Passenger's airbag
    • Driver's heater duct
    • Passenger's dashboard tray
  1. Remove the fuel fill door opener.
  1. Pull the antenna lead (A) from the bottom of the dashboard.
  1. Remove the bolts (A) and screws (B, C) securing the dashboard (D) and steering hanger beam (E), disconnect the hazard warning switch connector (F), power mirror switch connector (G). Detach the harness clips (H), and separate the dashboard and steering hanger beam.
  1. Install the beam in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the dashboard wire harness is not pinched.
    • Make sure the connectors are plugged in properly.
    • Fuel fill door opener cable is surely passed through hole of the dashboard trim.
