A/C Receiver/Dryer Desiccant Replacement
  1. Remove the condensor.
  1. Remove the mount (A) and the cap (B) from the bottom of the condenser, then remove the O-rings (C), the filter (D) and the desiccant (E).
  1. Install the desiccant in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    Replace the O-rings with new ones, and apply a thin coat of refrigarent oil (KEIHIN SP-10) before installing them. Be sure to use the right O-rings for HFC-134a (R-134a) to avoid leakage.
