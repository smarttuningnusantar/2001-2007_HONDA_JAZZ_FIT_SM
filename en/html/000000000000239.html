Taillight Replacement
  1. Remove the rear bumper.
  1. Open the tailgate and remove the maintenance lid.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) from the taillight (B).
    Brake/Taillight:
    21/5 W
    Turn Signal Light:
    21 W
    Back-Up Light:
    21 W
    Rear Fog Light:
    21 W (KG,KR and KE models)

  1. Remove the mounting nuts and the screw, then remove the taillight.
  1. When installing the taillight, check for the gasket; if it is distorted or stays compressed, replace it.
  1. After installing the taillight, run water over the taillight to make sure they do not leak.
