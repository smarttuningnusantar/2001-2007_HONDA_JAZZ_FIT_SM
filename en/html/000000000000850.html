A/T Shift Lock Solenoid Replacement
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Remove the A/T gear position indicator panel light socket (A) from the indicator panel (B), then remove the bulb (C) from the socket.
  1. Remove the shift lock solenoid/park pin switch connector (D) from the shift lever bracket base (E), then remove the connector.
  1. Remove the park pin switch connector (F).
  1. Release the lock tab (G) securing the shift lock solenoid, then remove the shift lock solenoid (H).
  1. Install the shift lock solenoid plunger (I) and spring (J) in the new shift lock solenoid.
  1. Install the shift lock solenoid by aligning the joint (K) of the shift lock solenoid plunger with the tip of the shift lock stop (L).
  1. Install the A/T gear position indicator panel light bulb in the panel light socket, then install the panel light socket in the A/T gear position indicator panel.
  1. Route the harness (L) through the shift lever bracket base, and connect the shift lock solenoid/park pin switch connector, then install it on the bracket base.
  1. Connect the park pin switch connector.
  1. Check that the shift lock releases when the shift lock release is pushed, and check that it locks when the shift lock release is released.
  1. Install the center console.
