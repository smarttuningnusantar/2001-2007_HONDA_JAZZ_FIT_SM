Front Bumper Lower Grille Replacement
Normal Type
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the bumper lower grille (A):
    -1.
    Remove the screws (B) and clips (C) from the back of the front bumper (D).
    -2.
    Remove the bumper lower grille from the bumper by releasing all of the hooks (E).
  1. Install the grille in the reverse order of removal. Push the hook portions into place securely.
