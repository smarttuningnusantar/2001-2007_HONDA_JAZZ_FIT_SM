Brake Hoses and Lines Inspection
  1. Inspect the brake hoses, for damage, deterioration, leaks, interference, and twisting.
  1. Check the brake lines for damage, rusting, and leakage. Also check for bent brake lines.
  1. Check for leaks at hose and line joints or connections, and retighten if necessary.
  1. Check the master cylinder and ABS modulator unit (if equipped) for damage and leakage.
    NOTE: Replace the brake hose clip whenever the brake hose is serviced.
