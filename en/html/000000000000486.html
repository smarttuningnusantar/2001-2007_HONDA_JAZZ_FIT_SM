ATF Strainer and Main Valve Body Removal
  1. Remove the ATF passage pipe (A).
  1. Remove the ATF strainer (B).
  1. Remove the ATF pass body (C), dowel pins (D), and separator plate (E).
  1. Remove the start clutch accumulator valve spring (F) and valve (G), then remove the main valve body (H) and dowel pins (I).
  1. Remove the ATF magnet (J), and clean it, then reinstall it in the flywheel housing (K).
  1. Replace the O-rings (L) with new ones, and install the all parts in the reverse order of removal.
