Rear Door Rear Sash Trim and Rear Door Glass Outer Weatherstrip Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Lower the glass fully.
  1. Remove the door quarter inner trim.
  1. From inside the door, remove the screw.
  1. Pull up the door rear sash trim (B) with the rear of the door glass outer weatherstrip (C) to release the hooks (D), then remove it from the door and the weatherstrip. Do not remove the weatherstrip at this time.
  1. While lifting the rear of the door glass outer weatherstrip, slide the weatherstrip rearward until the font clip (B) touches the inside of the door.
  1. Twist the door glass outer weatherstrip (A) to pull the front clip (B) out from the inside of the door, then remove the weatherstrip.
  1. Push the clip portions of the door glass outer weatherstrip into place securely.
  1. Install the rear sash trim in the reverse order of removal.
