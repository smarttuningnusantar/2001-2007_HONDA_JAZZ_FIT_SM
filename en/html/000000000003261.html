DTC Troubleshooting: 1-4
DTC 1-4:
 Short to Power in Driver's Airbag Inflator
Special Tools Required

SRS inflator simulator
 07SAZ-TB4011A

SRS simulator lead F
 07XAZ-SZ30100
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the D1i connector from the D1o connector (A).
  1. Connect the special tool (2 Ω connector) to the D1o connector (A).
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 1-4 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Short to power in the driver's airbag; replace the driver's airbag. ■
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the U1o connector (A) from the SRS unit. Do not disconnect SRS simulator lead F from cable reel 4P connector.
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Disconnect the SRS inflator simulator (special tool) from the SRS simulator lead F.
  1. Check for voltage between each terminal of SRS simulator lead F and body ground. There should be 0.5 V or less.
Is the voltage as specified?
YES -
Faulty SRS unit ; replace the SRS unit. ■
NO -
Go to Step 15 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the D2o connector (A) from the D2i connector (B).
  1. Check for voltage between each terminal of SRS simulator lead F and body ground. There should be 0.5 V or less.
Is the voltage as specified?
YES -
Replace the dashboard wire harness.■
NO -
