NO -

  1. Disconnect the radiator fan motor 2P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 1 terminal of the radiator fan relay 5P socket and the No. 2 terminal of the radiator fan motor 2P connector.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the under-hood fuse/relay box and the radiator fan motor 2P connector terminal No. 2.■
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 1 terminal of the radiator fan motor 2P connector and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the radiator fan motor.■
NO -
Check for an open in the wire between radiator fan motor 2P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground. If the wire is OK, check for a poor ground at G301.■
