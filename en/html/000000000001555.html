DTC Troubleshooting: P2227 (13)
DTC P2227:
 BARO Sensor Range/Performance Problem
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the BARO SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is atmospheric pressure indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Do the DTC CLEAR in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 3,000 rpm (min −1 ) with no load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Test-drive under following conditions.
    • Engine Coolant temperature more than 158°F (70°C)
    • Transmission in 2nd position
    • Vehicle speed 0 km/h (0 mph) to 60 km/h (38 mph) with fully acceleration
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P2227 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is it FAILED?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
If it is PASSED, intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. If it is NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 4 and recheck.
  1. Substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P2227 indicated, check for poor connections at the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Replace the original ECM. ■
