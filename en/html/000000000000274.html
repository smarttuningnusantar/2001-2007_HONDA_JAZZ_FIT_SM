Rearview Mirror Replacement
NOTE: When prying with a flat-tip screwdriver, wrap it with protective tape to prevent damage.
  1. Using a flat-tip screwdriver, carefully remove the headliner cap (A) by releasing the hooks (B) on the front edge, and pull downward. Take care not to scratch the cap and headliner (C).
  1. Remove the screws and mirror holder (A), then remove the rearview mirror (B).
  1. Install the mirror in the reverse order of removal.
