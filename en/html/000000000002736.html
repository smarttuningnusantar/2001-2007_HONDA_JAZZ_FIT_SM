Idle Speed Inspection
NOTE:
  • Leave the idle air control (IAC) valve connected.
  • Before checking the idle speed, check these items:
    • -
    The malfunction indicator lamp (MIL) has not been reported on.
    -
    Ignition timing
    -
    Spark plugs
    -
    Air cleaner
    -
    PCV system
  1. Disconnect the evaporative emission (EVAP) canister purge valve 2P connector.
  1. Connect a tachometer (A) to the test tachometer connector (B), or connect the HDS or a scan tool to the data link connector (DLC) (C) located under the driver's side of the dashboard.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 3,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in Park or neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Check the idle speed without load conditions: headlights, blower fan, rear defogger, radiator fan, and air conditioner are not operating.
    Idle speed should be:
    750±50 rpm (min −1 ) (in Park or neutral)

  1. Idle the engine for 1 minute with A/C switch ON, temperature control at maximum cool, blower fan at maximum speed, rear window defogger ON,headlights at high beam, then check the idle speed.
    Idle speed should be:
    850±50 rpm (min −1 ) (in Park or neutral)

    NOTE: If the idle speed is not within specification, do the symptom troubleshooting.
  1. Reconnect the EVAP canister purge valve 2P connector.
    NOTE: You can use the HDS or a scan tool to inspect idle speed.
