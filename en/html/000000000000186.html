Cowl Covers Replacement
  1. Remove the windshield wiper arms.
  1. Remove the hood rear seal (A) by pulling it out. Using a clip remover, detach the clips (B) from the cowl covers. Take care not to scratch the cowl covers.
  1. Detach the clips (D, E) by carefully pulling the passenger's cowl cover (F) upward, and release the hook (G) on the passenger's cowl cover from the drivers, then remove the cover. Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Disconnect the windshield washer tubes (B).
  1. Detach the clips (D, E) by carefully pulling the driver's cowl cover upward, then remove the cover. Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Apply sealant (Cemedine P/N. 08712-0004 or equivalent) (I) in the groove of the hood rear seal from end to end.
  1. Install the covers in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Make sure the washer tubes are connected securely.
