CVT Flywheel Housing Input Shaft Oil Seal Replacement
Special Tools Required

Handle driver
 07749-0010000

Driver attachment, 37 x 40 mm
 07746-0010200
  1. Remove the main valve body from the flywheel housing.
  1. Remove the old oil seal from the flywheel housing.
  1. Install the new oil seal flush with the flywheel housing. It is allowed to install the oil seal plus or minus 0.5 mm (0.02 in.) from level.
