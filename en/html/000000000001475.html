A/C Signal Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Turn the blower switch ON.
  1. Turn the A/C switch ON.
  1. Check the A/C CLUTCH in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is it ON?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Go to the A/C pressure switch circuit troubleshooting.
  1. Check the A/C system.
Does the A/C system operate?
YES -
The air conditioning system circuit is OK.■
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Activate the A/C CLUTCH in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is there clicking noise from the A/C compressor clutch?
YES -
Go to the A/C section, and look for the other symptoms.■
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Momentarily connect the A/C compressor clutch relay 4P connector (into the under-hood fuse/relay box) termminal No. 3 to body ground with a jumper wire several times.
Is there a clicking noise from the A/C compressor clutch?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM connector No. 4 terminal No. 19 (test pin box terminal No. 100) and the A/C clutch relay.■
NO -
Substitute a known-good ECM and recheck. If symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM .■
