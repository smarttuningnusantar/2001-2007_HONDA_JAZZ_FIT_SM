ABS Indicator Circuit Troubleshooting
ABS indicator does not come on
  1. Do the gauge assembly self-diagnosis function.
Is gauge assembly OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace gauge assembly. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the gauge assembly.
  1. Disconnect the gauge assembly connector C (40P).
  1. Check for continuity between gauge assembly connector C (40P) terminal No. 26 and the body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the gauge assembly and the VSA modulator-control unit.■
NO -
Check for loose or poor connections in the gauge assembly connectors. If connections are good, replace the gauge assembly. ■
ABS indicator does not go off, and no DTCs are stored
  1. Check the No. 25 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck. If the fuse is blown, check for a short to body ground in this fuse circuit. If the circuit is OK, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
