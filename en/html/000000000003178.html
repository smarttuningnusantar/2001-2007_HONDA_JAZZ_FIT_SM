VSA OFF Switch Test
  1. Remove the VSA OFF Switch (A) from the switch panel.
  1. Disconnect the VSA OFF Switch 5P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA OFF Switch 5P connector terminal No. 1 and No. 2. There should be continuity when the switch is pushed.
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA OFF Switch connector terminal No. 4 and No. 5. There should be continuity at all times.
