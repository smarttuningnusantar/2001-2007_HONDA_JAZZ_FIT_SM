Engine Oil/Air Separator Installation
  1. Clean and dry the oil/air separator mating surfaces, bolts and bolt holes.
  1. Apply a 1.5 mm wide bead of the liquid gasket, P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M or 08C70-X0331S, along the broken line (A).
    NOTE: Do not install the parts if 5 minutes or more have elapsed since applying liquid gasket. Instead, reapply liquid gasket after removing old residue.
  1. Install the oil/air separator.
