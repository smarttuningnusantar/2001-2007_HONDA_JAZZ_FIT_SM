DTC 51:
 Motor Lock
  1. Check the No. 4 (40A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box, and reinstall the fuse if it is OK.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck.■

Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
