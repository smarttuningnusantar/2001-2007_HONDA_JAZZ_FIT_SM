Sunroof Limit Switch Test
OPEN/CLOSE, TILT/CLOSE SWITCH:
  1. Remove the headliner
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector from the sunroof limit switch.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the table.
    NOTE: Turn the motor by hand with the wrench.
OPEN SWITCH (KK, KU and KT models):
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector from the sunroof limit switch.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the table.
    NOTE: Turn the motor by hand with the wrench.
