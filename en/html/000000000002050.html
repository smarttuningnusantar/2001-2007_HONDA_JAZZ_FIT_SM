7-speed Mode Main Switch Replacement
For KB, KE, KG, and KQ (L15A1 engine) Models (6-position CVT+7Speed Mode)
SRS components are located in this area. Review the following articles in the SRS before performing repairs or service;
  • Component Locations.
  • Precautions and Procedures.
  1. Remove the steering wheel.
  1. Remove the screws (A) (4) securing the steering wheel rear cover (B), then remove the steering wheel rear cover.
  1. Remove steering shift + (plus) switch connector from its clamp, and disconnect the connector.
  1. Remove the screw (C) securing the steering shift + (plus) switch (D), then remove the switch.
  1. Disconnect 7SPEED MODE main switch connector, and remove the 7SPEED MODE main switch assembly (E).
  1. Replace the 7SPEED MODE main switch with a new one.
  1. Install the new 7SPEED MODE main switch and steering shift + (plus) switch with the screw, and install the steering wheel rear cover with the screws (4).
  1. Install the steering wheel.
