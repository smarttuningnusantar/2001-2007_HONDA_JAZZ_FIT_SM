High/Low Beam Switching Solenoid Test
  1. Remove the headlight.
  1. Remove the HID bulb from the headlight.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the high/low beam switching solenoid (B).
  1. Check the hood of the high/low beam switching solenoid.
    • The hood should open (high beam) when the battery power is connected to the No. 1 terminal and ground the No. 2 terminal.
    • The hood should close (low beam) when the battery power is disconnected.
  1. If the solenoid does not work properly, replace it.
