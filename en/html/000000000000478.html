CVT Input Shaft Planetary Carrier Clearance Inspection
  1. Measure the clearance between the 25 x 31 mm thrust shim (A) and the snap ring (B) with a feeler gauge (C), in at least three places. Use the average as the actual clearance.
    STANDARD:
    0.05−0.115 mm (0.018−0.030 in.)

  1. If the clearance is out of standard, remove the 25 x 31 mm thrust shim (A) and measure its thickness.
  1. Select and install the new thrust shim, and secure them with the snap ring, then recheck.
