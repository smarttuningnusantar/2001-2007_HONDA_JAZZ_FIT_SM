DTC Troubleshooting: 11, 13, 15, 17
DTC 11, 13, 15, 17:
 Wheel Sensor (Open/Short to Body Ground/Short to Power)
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Disconnect the ABS control unit 25P connector.
  1. Reconnect the battery cable.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and the appropriate wheel sensor + B and GND terminals of the ABS control unit 25P connector individually (see table).
    DTC
    Appropriate Terminal
    + B
    GND

    RR − GND:No. 23
    17 (Left-rear)
    RL + B:No. 6
    RL − GND:No. 5
