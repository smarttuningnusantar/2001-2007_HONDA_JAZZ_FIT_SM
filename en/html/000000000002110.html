DTC Troubleshooting: 67
DTC 67:
 Brake Pedal Position Switch (Hardwear)
  1. Check the brake pedal position switch.
Is the switch OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the brake pedal position switch.■
  1. Disconnect the VSA control unit 46P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and the VSA control unit 46P connector terminal No. 6 when the brake pedal is released.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the VSA control unit and under-hood fuse/relay box.■
NO -
Replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
