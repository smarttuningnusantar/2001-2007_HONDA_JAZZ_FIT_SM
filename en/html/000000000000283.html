Symptom Troubleshooting: No picture is shown on the display
No picture is shown on the display
  1. Check the No. 8 (15A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Reinstall the fuse, and go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck.■
  1. Check the No. 17 (7.5A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Reinstall the fuse, and go to Step 3 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and AVN unit connector A (12P) terminals No. 1 and No. 2 individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the fuse/relay box and the AVN unit.■
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and AVN unit connector B (20P) terminals No. 2 and No. 10.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the fuse/relay box and the AVN unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between AVN unit connector A (12P) terminal No. 4 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the AVN unit and body ground (G403).■
  1. Check for continuity between AVN unit connector B (20P) terminal No. 20 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the AVN unit and body ground (G403).■
  1. Perform the forced starting of display.
Is the diagnosis menu of the picture diagnosis displayed?
YES -
Perform the System Links Test in the Picture Diagnosis Test menu. ■
NO -
Replace the AVN unit.■
