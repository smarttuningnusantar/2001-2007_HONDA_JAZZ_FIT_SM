Subfuel Tank Low Fuel Indicator Circuit Troubleshooting (Subfuel tank low fuel indicator does not come on)
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and watch the indicators except subfuel tank low fuel indicator.
Is it normal?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Check the gauge assembly circuit, if the circuit is OK, replace the gauge assembly. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. With connector connected, connect fuel indicator unit 5P connector terminal No. 2 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the subfuel tank low fuel indicator.
Is the subfuel tank low fuel indicator indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the fuel indicator unit 5P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between fuel indicator unit 5P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the fuel indicator unit and No. 3 METER (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box. ■
  1. Measure voltage between fuel indicator unit 5P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 5.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Replace the fuel indicator unit. ■
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the fuel indicator unit and G401.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. With connector connected, connect gauge assembly 30P connector terminal No. 25 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the subfuel tank low fuel indicator.
Is the subfuel tank low fuel indicator indicated?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the fuel indicator unit and the gauge assembly. ■
NO -
Replace the original gauge assembly. ■
