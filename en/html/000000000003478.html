Subfuel Tank Low Fuel Indicator Circuit Troubleshooting (Subfuel tank low fuel indicator stays on with enough fuel)
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and watch the subfuel tank low fuel indicator.
Does the subfuel tank low fuel indicator stay on for more than 2 seconds after turning the ignition switch ON (II)?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. With connector connected, measure voltage between fuel indicator unit 5P connector terminal No. 4 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Replace the fuel indicator unit.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Roll up the right front inner fender.
  1. Disconnect the fuel/satellite sensor subharness 4P connector (A).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between fuel/satellite sensor subharness 4P connector terminal No. 3 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the fuel indicator unit (sensor out line) and the fuel subharness.■
  1. Measure voltage between fuel/satellite sensor subharness 4P connector terminal No. 1 and No. 3.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
