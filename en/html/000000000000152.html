Front Impact Sensor Replacement
Removal
NOTE:
  • Removal of the front sensor must be performed according to the precautions/procedures described before.
  • Before disconnecting the front sensor 2P connector(s), disconnect the driver's and front passenger's airbag 2P, both seat belt tensioner 4P connector(s).
  • Do not turn the ignition switch ON (II) and do not connect the battery cable while replacing the front sensor.
  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Remove the front inner fender.
  1. Disconnect the engine compartment wire harness (or SRS front sensor wire harness) 2P connector (A), and remove the two Torx bolts (B) using a Torx T30 bit, then remove the front sensor (C).
Installation
NOTE:
  • Be sure to install the harness wires so that they are not pinched or interfering with other parts.
  • Do not turn the ignition switch ON (II) and do not connect the battery cable while replacing the front sensor.
  1. Install the new front sensor with new Torx bolts (A), then connect the engine compartment wire harness (or SRS front sensor wire harness) 2P connector (B) to the front sensor (C).
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. After installing the front sensor, confirm proper system operation: Turn the ignition switch ON (II): the SRS indicator should come on for about 6 seconds and then go off.
