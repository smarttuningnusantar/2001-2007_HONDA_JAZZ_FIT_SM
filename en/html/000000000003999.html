DTC Troubleshooting: 20
DTC 20:
 ELD Circuit Malfunction
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM.
  1. Start the engine and keep engine speed at idle.
  1. Turn the headlights on.
Does the MIL indicate DTC 20?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ELD and at the ECM / PCM .■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Start the engine and let it idle.
  1. Measure voltage between ECM/PCM connector terminals A24 and E15.
  1. While measuring voltage between ECM/PCM connector terminals A24 and E15, turn the headlights on (low).
Does the voltage drop when the headlights are turned on?
YES -
Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch and headlights OFF.
  1. Disconnect the ELD 3P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the ELD 3P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Measure voltage between ELD 3P connector terminal No. 3 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Replace the ELD.■
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Connect the ELD 3P connector terminal No. 3 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and ECM/PCM connector terminal E15.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM / PCM (E15) and the ELD.■
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and ECM/PCM connector terminal E15.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM / PCM (E15) and the ELD.■
NO -
Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
  1. Measure voltage between the ELD 3P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ELD and G201.■
NO -
Check the No. 16 (7.5A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box. If the fuse is OK, repair open in the wire between No. 16 (7.5A) fuse and the ELD.■
