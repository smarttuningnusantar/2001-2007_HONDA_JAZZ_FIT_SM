DTC Troubleshooting: 11, 13, 15, 17
DTC 11, 13, 15, 17:
 Wheel Sensor (Short to Power/Short to Body Ground/Open)
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the DTCs using the HDS.
Does the VSA and ABS indicators come on, and are DTCs 11, 13, 15, 17 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check that connectors are installed correctly in the VSA control unit 46P connector and the wheel sensor 2P connectors.
Are connectors installed correctly?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Reconnect the connector(s) correctly, and recheck.■

Is there continuity in both directions?
YES -
Repair the wires between the VSA modulator-control unit and the wheel sensor that are shorted to each other.■
NO -
Check for loose or poor connections at the VSA control unit 46P connector and the appropriate wheel sensor 2P connector. If the connection are good, replace the appropriate wheel sensor, and recheck for DTCs. If the DTC returns, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
