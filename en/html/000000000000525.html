Front Fender Fairing Replacement
  1. Remove the front inner fender as necessary.
  1. Open the front door. From inside the door, remove the clips securing the front fender fairing (A) and front fender.
  1. From the wheel arch, remove the clip (A), and release the clip (B), then remove the front fender fairing (C).
  1. Install the fender fairing in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Before installing the clips of the door upper and lower portions, install the front fender fairing to the front fender (D) properly as shown.
