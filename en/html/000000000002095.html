DTC Troubleshooting: 21
DTC 21:
 Wheel Slipping
  1. Check that all four tyres are the specified size, and are inflated to the proper specification.
Are all four tyres the correct size and properly inflated?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Install the correct tyres or set the tyres to the correct inflation, and retest.■
  1. Check that all wheel sensors are installed correctly.
Are wheel sensors installed correctly?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Reinstall or replace the wheel sensor(s). ■
  1. Check all wheel bearings.
Are they OK?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections in the VSA control unit 46P connector. If connections are good, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
Replace the wheel bearing(s). ■
