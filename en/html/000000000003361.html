DTC Troubleshooting: 11-01, 11-02, 12-01
DTC 11-01:
 IG1 Voltage (Initial check)
DTC 11-02:
 IG1 Voltage (Outside Range Check)
DTC 12-01:
 VBU check
NOTE: Before you troubleshooting, check for No. 16 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Clear the DTC.
  1. Disconnect the EPS control unit connector B (16P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II)
  1. Measure the voltage between the EPS control unit connector B (16P) terminal No. 8 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the fuse box and the EPS control unit.■
  1. Check the battery.
Is the battery OK?
YES -
Check for loose the EPS control unit connectors. If necessary, replace the EPS control unit and recheck it.■
NO -
The battery is replaced or charged.■
