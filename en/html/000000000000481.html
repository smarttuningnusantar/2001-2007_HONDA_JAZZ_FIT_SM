A/T Key Interlock Solenoid Test
  1. Disconnect the key switch connector.
  1. Insert the ignition key in the key cylinder, and turn the ignition switch ACC (I).
  1. Connect the battery positive terminal to key switch connector terminal No. 4, and connect the battery negative terminal to the terminal No. 3, then check that the ignition key is turned to LOCK (0) and removed from the key cylinder.
  1. If the ignition key cannot be turned and removed from the key cylinder, replace the ignition key cylinder/steering lock assembly.
