DTC Troubleshooting: P1891 (43-1)
DTC P1891:
 Problem in Start Clutch Control System
NOTE: Record all freeze data before you troubleshoot.
  1. Check whether the another DTC is indicated.
Is the another DTC indicated?
YES -
Perform the troubleshooting for the indicated DTC(s). Recheck for DTC P1891 after troubleshooting.
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect CVT start clutch pressure control valve connector from the control valve body.
  1. Start the engine, and shift to [D] position.
  1. Check if the vehicle moves.
Does the vehicle move?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Replace the start clutch.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Reconnect CVT start clutch pressure control valve connector.
  1. Start the engine, and shift to [D] position.
  1. Check if the vehicle creeps and check the creeping speed on level ground.
Does the vehicle creep and is the creeping speed about 5 km/h (3 mph)?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Replace the control valve body.
  1. Measure the stall speed in [D], [S], [L], and [R] position.
Is the stall speed over 3,500 rpm (min −1 )?
YES -
Replace the start clutch.■
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect CVT start clutch pressure control valve connector from the control valve body.
  1. Start the engine, and shift to [D] position.
  1. Check if the vehicle moves.
Does the vehicle move?
YES -
The system is OK, update the PCM if it does not have the latest software, or subsutitute a known-good PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/ indication goes away with a known-good PCM , replace the original PCM.■
NO -
Replace the start clutch.■
