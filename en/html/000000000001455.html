Alternator Replacement
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable from the battery.
  1. Remove the intake manifold.
  1. Disconnect the alternator connector (A), WHT wire (B) and harness clamp (C) from the alternator.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Remove the lock bolt (A) and mounting bolt (B), then remove the alternator.
  1. Install the alternator in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Adjust the drive belt tension.
  1. Perform the ETCS learn procedure (with ETCS).
