DTC Troubleshooting: 3-1
DTC 3-1:
 Open or Increased Resistance in Left Side Seat Belt Tensioner
Special Tools Required

SRS inflator simulator
 07SAZ-TB4011A

SRS simulator lead F
 07XAZ-SZ30100
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the TL1o connector from the TL1i connector (A).
  1. Connect the special tool (2 Ω connector) to the TL1i connector (A).
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 3-1 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Open or increased resistance in the left side seat belt tensioner; replace the left side seat belt. ■
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the TR1o connector (A) from the TR1i connector (B).
  1. Disconnect the U2o connector (A) from the SRS unit.
  1. Check resistance between the No. 1 terminal and the No. 2 terminal of U2o connector. There should be 2.0-3.0 Ω.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Faulty SRS unit or poor contact at U2o connector and the SRS unit , check the connection between the U2o connector and the SRS unit . If the connection is OK, replace the SRS unit. ■
NO -
Open or increased resistance in the floor wire harness; replace the floor wire harness.■
