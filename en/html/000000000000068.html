Front Stabilizer Bar Replacement
  1. Raise the front of the vehicle, and make sure it is securely supported. Remove the front wheels.
  1. Remove the stabilizer links on right and left side.
  1. Remove the rear beam (A).
  1. Remove the bushing holders (B), then remove the bushings (C) and the stabilizer bar (D).
  1. Install the stabilizer bar in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Note the right and left direction of the stabilizer bar.
    • Align the ends of the paint marks (A) on the stabilizer bar with the bushings (B).
    • Refer to Stabilizer Link Replacement to connect the stabilizer bar to the links.
  1. Install the rear beam.
