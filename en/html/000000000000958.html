High/Low Beam Switching Solenoid Replacement
  1. Remove the headlight.
  1. Remove the HID bulb from the headlight.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the high/low beam switching solenoid (B).
  1. Turn the headlight adjuster (C) about eight turns counterclockwise.
  1. Place a clean shop towel or paper (D) under the high/low beam switching solenoid.
  1. Remove the two screws from the high/low beam switching solenoid (A).
  1. Disconnect the 1P terminal (B) and remove the high/low beam switching solenoid.
    NOTE: Do not touch the hood and the moving part. It is necessary to clean if touch the parts.
  1. Install the switching solenoid in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After replacement, check the hood operation and adjust the headlight.
