Dust and Pollen (A/C) Filter Replacement
NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is symmetrical.
The dust and pollen filter should be replaced every 30,000 km (18,000 miles) or 12 months whichever comes first. Replace the filter more often if the air flow is less than usual.
  1. Remove the inner glove box.
  1. Remove the filter lid from the blower unit.
  1. Remove the filter (A) from the filter lid (B), and replace it.
  1. Install the filter in the reverse order of removal.
