DTC Troubleshooting: 61
DTC 61:
 IG1 Voltage Low
  1. Check the battery.
Is the battery OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the battery, then recheck.■
  1. Check the charging system.
Is the charging system OK?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Repair the charging system, then recheck.■
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle at 15 km/h (9 mph) or more.
  1. Check the DTCs using the HDS.
Does the ABS indicator come on, and are DTC 61 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections in the VSA control unit 46P connector. If connections are good, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
