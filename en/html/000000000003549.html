DTC Troubleshooting: P0462 (121)
DTC P0462:
 Fuel Level Sensor (Fuel Gauge Sending Unit) Circuit Low Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS, and wait 5 seconds.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0462 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly, the fuel gauge sending unit and ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Remove the access panel from the floor.
  1. Disconnect the fuel tank unit 4P connector.
  1. Remove the gauge assembly.
  1. Disconnect the gauge assembly 20P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 3 (52P).
  1. Check for continuity between fuel tank unit 4P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM connector No. 5 terminal No. 28 (test pin box terminal No. 57) and the fuel tank unit, the gauge assembly, then go to Step 25 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Connect the ECM test harness and the test pin box to the wire harness only, not to the ECM.
  1. Connect fuel tank unit 4P connector terminal No. 1 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between test pin box terminal No. 57 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM connector No. 3 terminal No. 28 (test pin box terminal No. 57) and the fuel tank unit, the gauge assembly, then go to Step 25 .
  1. Remove the fuel tank unit.
  1. Test the fuel gauge sending unit.
Is the fuel gauge sending unit OK?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Replace the fuel gauge sending unit, then go to Step 25 .
  1. Reconnect the gauge control module 20P connector.
  1. Reconnect the fuel tank unit 4P connector.
  1. Reconnect ECM connector No. 3 (52P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Set the float (A) to the E position (B).
  1. Check the fuel gauge.
Does the gauge move to the empty position?
YES -
Go to Step 31 .
NO -
Replace the gauge assembly, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Do the ECM RESET in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0462 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly, the fuel gauge sending unit and ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCsMENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0462 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly, the fuel gauge sending unit and ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Replace the original ECM. ■
