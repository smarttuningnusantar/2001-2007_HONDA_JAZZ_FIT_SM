7-speed Mode Steering Shift - (Minus) Switch Test
For KB, KE, KG, and KQ (L15A1 engine) Models (6-position CVT+7Speed Mode)
SRS components are located in this area. Review the following articles in the SRS before performing repairs or service;
  • Component Locations.
  • Precautions and Procedures.
  1. Remove the steering wheel.
  1. Check the steering shift − (minus) switch for continuity between cable reel subharness 13P connector terminals No. 7 and No. 9.
    There should be continuity while pressing the steering shift − (minus) switch, and no continuity when the switch is released.
  1. Steering shift − (minus) switch test has finished if the test result is OK.
    If the switch works incorrectly at the 13P connector, test the switch at the switch connector.
  1. Remove steering shift − (minus) switch connector from its clamp, and disconnect the connector.
  1. Check the steering shift − (minus) switch for continuity between the switch connector terminals while pressing the steering shift − (minus) switch and when the switch is released.
    There should be continuity while pressing the steering shift − (minus) switch, and no continuity when the switch is released.
  1. Replace the steering shift − (minus) switch if the switch works incorrectly.
  1. Replace the cable reel subharness if the switch is OK.
