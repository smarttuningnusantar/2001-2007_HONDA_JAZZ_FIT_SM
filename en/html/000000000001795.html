DTC Troubleshooting: P0563 (34-2)
DTC P0563 (34-2):
 ECM/PCM Power Source Circuit Unexpected Voltage
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Wait 5 seconds.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Is DTC P0563 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose wires at the No. 20 (15A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box and at the ECM / PCM .■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Reconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Measure voltage between ECM/PCM connector terminal E7 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 13 .
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Remove the driver's side under cover.
  1. Remove the relay holder (A) from the under-dash fuse/relay box, and remove the PGM-FI main relay 1 (brown) (B) from the relay holder (A), after reinstall the relay holder (A) to the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal E7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM/PCM (E7) and the PGM-FI main relay 1.■
NO -
Replace the PGM-FI main relay 1.■
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Reconnect ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Reconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Measure voltage between ECM/PCM connector terminal E7 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 18 .
NO -
Substitute a known-good ECM/PCM and recheck. If symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector A (31P).
  1. Reconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Measure voltage between body ground and ECM/PCM connector terminals A3 and A2 individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 21 .
NO -
Substitute a known-good ECM/PCM and recheck. If symptom/indication goes away, replace the original ECM / PCM .■
