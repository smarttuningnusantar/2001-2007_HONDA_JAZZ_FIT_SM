Power Mirror Replacement
  1. Raise the door glass fully.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Door panel
    • Plastic cover, as necessary
  1. Disconnect the connector (A).
  1. Detach the harness clip (B).
  1. Remove the maintenance cap (A), and remove the nuts. While holding the mirror, detach the clip (B), then remove the mirror (C). Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Install the mirror in the reverse order of removal, and make sure the connector is plugged in properly.
