Memorizing the EPS Torque Sensor Neutral Position
The torque sensor neutral position must be memorized whenever the gearbox is removed or installed, or when the torque sensor or EPS control unit is replaced. Note that the torque sensor neutral position is not affected when erasing the DTC.
  1. With the ignition switch OFF, connect the HDS to the 16P data link connector (DLC) (A) located under the driver's side of the dashboard.
  1. With the vehicle on the ground, set the front wheels in the straight ahead driving position.
  1. Short the SCS circuit to body ground using the HDS.
  1. Do the memorizing the torque sensor neutral position follow the HDS commands.
