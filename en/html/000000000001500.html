DTC Troubleshooting: P0118 (6)
DTC P0118:
 ECT Sensor Circuit High Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the ECT SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about −40°C (−40°F) or less, or 4.87 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time.Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECT sensor and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the ECT sensor 2P connector.
  1. Connect the ECT sensor 2P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2 with a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the ECT SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about −40°C (−40°F) or less, or 4.87 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 15 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the ECT sensor 2P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM connector No. 3 terminal No. 33 (test pin box terminal No. 62) and the ECT sensor, then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 10 seconds.
  1. Connect the ECM test harness and the test pin box between the ECM and connector.
  1. Measure voltage between test pin box terminal No. 81 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM connector No. 3 terminal No. 52 (test pin box terminal No. 81) and the ECT sensor, then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 22 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the ECT sensor.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Do the ECM RESET in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0118 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECT sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0118 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECT sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Replace the original ECM. ■
