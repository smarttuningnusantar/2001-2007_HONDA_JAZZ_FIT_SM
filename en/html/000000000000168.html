Audio Unit Removal/Installation
  1. Remove the center panel.
  1. Disconnect the 20P connector and antenna lead from the audio unit.
  1. Disconnect the connectors from the heater control panel.
  1. Disconnect the connector from the rear window defogger switch.
  1. Remove the seven mounting screws, then remove the audio unit (A).
  1. Remove the four mounting screws from the LCD unit (A), then remove the cover (B).
  1. Remove the two knobs (A).
  1. Remove the sixteen mounting screw from the printed circuit board (B), then remove the printed circuit board and LCD unit.
  1. Install the reverse order of removal.
    NOTE: Do not remove the connection of the printed circuit board and the LCD unit.
