7-speed Mode Main Switch/Steering Shift Switch Replacement
SRS components are located in this area. Review the following articles in the SRS section before performing repairs or service;
  • Component Locations.
  • Precautions and Procedures.
  1. Remove the driver's airbag from the steering wheel.
  1. Disconnect the main switch (7SPEED MODE)/steering shift switch connector (A).
  1. Remove the screw covers (B), then remove the screws.
  1. Remove the switch (C), and replace the switch with the new one.
  1. Connect the switch connector, insert it in the connector holder, and route the harnesses along the harness guide.
  1. Install the driver's airbag.
