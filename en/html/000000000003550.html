DTC Troubleshooting: P0463 (121)
DTC P0463:
 Fuel Level Sensor (Fuel Gauge Sending Unit) Circuit High Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS, and wait 5 seconds.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0463 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly, the fuel gauge sending unit, and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Remove the access panel from the floor.
  1. Disconnect the fuel tank unit 4P connector.
  1. Disconnect the gauge assembly 20P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 3 (52P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between fuel tank unit 4P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the ECM connector No. 3 terminal No. 28 (test pin box terminal No. 57) and the fuel tank unit, the gauge assembly, then go to Step 15 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the fuel tank unit.
  1. Test the fuel gauge sending unit.
Is the fuel gauge sending unit OK?
YES -
Go to Step 22 .
NO -
Replace the fuel gauge sending unit, then go to Step 16 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Do the ECM RESET in the CLEAR MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0463 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly, the fuel gauge sending unit and ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0463 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly, the fuel gauge sending unit and ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC troubleshooting.
NO -
Replace the original ECM. ■
