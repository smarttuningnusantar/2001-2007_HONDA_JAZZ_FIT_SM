Front Inner Fender Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove the front inner fender (A).
    -1.
    On the back of the wheel arch, remove the screws (B) and clip (C). If equipped, remove the front splash guard (D).
    -2.
    From under the front bumper (E), remove the screw (F) securing the front bumper, splash shield (G), and front inner fender, and remove the clip (H) securing the front bumper and front inner fender. With front air spoiler, remove the clip (I) securing the front air spoiler, front bumper, and front inner fender.
    -3.
    From the wheel arch, remove the bolt (J) (left side) and clips (K) securing the front inner fender (and splash shield) on the body.
    -4.
    Release the hook (L) of the splash shield, then remove the front inner fender.
  1. Install the inner fender in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
