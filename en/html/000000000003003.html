Keyless Entry Transmitter Programming
Storing transmitter codes:
The codes of up to three transmitters can be read into the keyless receiver unit memory. (If a fourth code is stored, the code which was input first will be erased.)
NOTE: It is important to maintain the time limits between the steps.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., push the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the key less receiver unit.
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., push the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the key less receiver unit.
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Within 4 sec., turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., push the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the key less receiver unit.
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Within 4 sec., turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Within 1 to 4 sec., push the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the key less receiver unit.
  1. Confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators. Within 1 to 4 sec., push the transmitter lock or unlock button again.
  1. Within 10 sec., aim the transmitters (up to three) whose codes you want to store at the receiver, and press the transmitter lock or unlock buttons.
    Confirm that you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators after each transmitter code is stored.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and pull out the key.
  1. Confirm proper operation with the new code(s).
