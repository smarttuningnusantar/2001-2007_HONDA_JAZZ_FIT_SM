Fuse Lid Assembly Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • LHD is shown, RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the fuse lid (A) by pulling it back.
  1. Remove the screws (B, C), release the clips (D) and hooks (E) by pulling the driver's tray trim (F) back, then remove it.
  1. Install the trim and lid in the reverse order of reassembly. Push the clip and hook portions into place securely.
