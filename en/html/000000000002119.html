DTC Troubleshooting: 91
DTC 91:
 F-CAN Communication
NOTE: Before performing this troubleshooting, troubleshoot the PGM-FI System first.
  1. Check for DTCs in the ECM.
Are any DTCs indicated?
YES -
Troubleshoot the ECM DTCs.■
NO -
Check for loose connector terminals in the VSA control unit 46P connector and repair if necessary. If terminals are OK, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
