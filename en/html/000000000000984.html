Windshield Wiper Motor Test
Windshield
  1. Remove the wiper arms, hood seals and cowl covers.
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector (A) from the wiper motor (B).
  1. Test the motor by connecting battery power to the No. 2 terminal and ground the No. 1 [No. 3] terminal of the wiper motor 5P connector. The motor should run. If the motor does not run or fails to run smoothly, replace the motor.
