A/T Park Pin Switch Test/Replacement
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Disconnect the park pin switch connector (2P).
  1. Shift the shift lever into the [P] position, then check for continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals. There should be continuity.
  1. Shift the shift lever out of the [P] position, and check for continuity between the terminals in Step 3 . There should be no continuity.
  1. If the park pin switch is faulty, remove the shift lever assembly, and replace the detent bracket. (The park pin switch is not available separately.)
