DTC Troubleshooting: 73
DTC 73:
 Yaw Rate Sensor (Power Supply)
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II).
  1. Verify the DTC.
Is DTC 73 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check the No. 25 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box, and reinstall the fuse if it is OK.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck.■
  1. Remove the No. 25 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse rate/relay box, and disconnect the VSA control unit 46P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector terminal No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body in the wire between the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor and under-dash fuse/relay box.■
NO -
Replace the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor. ■
