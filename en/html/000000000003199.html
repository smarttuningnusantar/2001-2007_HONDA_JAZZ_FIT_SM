In-car Temperature Sensor Replacement
NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is similar.
  1. Remove the gauge assembly.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) and the air hose (B). Remove the self-tapping screws, the in-car temperature sensor (C) and the bracket (D) (LHD model) from the dashboard.
  1. Install the sensor in the reverse order of removal. Be sure to connect the air hose securely.
