DTC Troubleshooting: 61-03
DTC 61-03:
 Motor Circuit (Open)
  1. Clear the DTC.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Turn the steering wheel from lock to lock several times, and wait for 10 seconds or more.
Does the EPS indicator turn off?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Stop the engine.
  1. Check the DTC.
Is DTC 61-03 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Do the troubleshooting for the DTC indicated.■
  1. Disconnect the EPS control unit connector A (7P) and the motor 2P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the EPS control unit connector A (7P) terminal No. 7 and the motor 2P connector terminal No. 1 or No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the EPS control unit and the motor.■
  1. Check for continuity between the EPS control unit connector A (7P) terminal No. 6 and the motor 2P connector terminal No. 2 or No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the EPS control unit and the motor.■
  1. Check for continuity between the motor terminal No. 1 and No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Check for loose the EPS control unit connectors. If necessary, replace the EPS control unit and recheck it.■
NO -
Replace the motor.■
