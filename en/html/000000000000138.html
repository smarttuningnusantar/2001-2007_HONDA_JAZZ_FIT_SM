Heater Control Panel Disassembly and Reassembly
NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the heater fan switch (A), the air mix control cable (B), the mode control cable (C), and the recirculation control cable (D).
  1. Widen the cable stops (A) in the cable holder (B) using a snap ring pliers, then pull out the control cable (C).
  1. Hook the tip of the new control cable to the lever or slider, then push the cable housing into the cable holder until it locks into place.
  1. After assembly, check that the lever slides or the dial turns smoothly through the full stroke from right to left.
