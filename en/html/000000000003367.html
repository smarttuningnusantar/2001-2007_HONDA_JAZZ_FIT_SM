DTC Troubleshooting: 34-01, 34-02
DTC 34-01:
 Power Relay Stuck ON
DTC 34-02:
 Fail Safe Relay Stuck ON
  1. Clear the DTC.
  1. Start the engine.
Does the EPS indicator turn off?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Stop the engine.
  1. Check the DTC.
Is DTC 34-01 or 34-02 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Do the troubleshooting for the DTC indicated.■
  1. Disconpect the EPS control unit connector A (7P) from the EPS control unit.
  1. Check for continuity between the EPS control unit connector A (7P) terminal No. 6 or No. 7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Disconnect the motor 2P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the motor 2P connector terminal No. 1 or No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the motor.■
NO -
Repair a short to body ground in the wire between the EPS control unit and the motor.■
  1. Disconnect the motor 2P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the EPS control unit connector A (7P) terminal No. 6 and No. 7.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair a short in the wire between the EPS control unit and the motor.■
NO -
Check for loose the EPS control unit connectors. If necessary, replace the EPS control unit and recheck it.■
