CVT Differential Backlash Inspection
  1. Install the drive shaft on the differential, then place the axles on V-blocks.
  1. Check the backlash of the pinion gears (A) with a dial indicator (B).
    STANDARD:
    0.05-0.15 mm (0.002-0.006 in.)

  1. If the backlash is out of standard, replace the differential carrier.
