DTC Troubleshooting: 52
DTC 52:
 Motor Stuck OFF
  1. Check the No. 4 (40A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box, and reinstall the fuse if it is OK.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck.■
  1. Disconnect the ABS control unit 25P connector.
  1. Measure the voltage between the ABS control unit 25P connector terminal No. 25 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Check for loose terminals in the ABS control unit 25P connector. If necessary, substitute a known-good ABS modulator-control unit , and recheck.■
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the No. 4 (40A) fuse and the ABS control unit.■
