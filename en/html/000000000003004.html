Keyless Receiver Unit Input Test
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard under cover.
  1. Remove the mounting nuts and down the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector (A) from the keyless receiver unit (B).
  1. Inspect the connector and socket terminals to be sure they are all making good contact.
    • If the terminal are bent, loose or corroded, repair them as necessary, and recheck the system.
    • If the terminals are OK, go to Step 5 .
  1. With the connector still disconnected, make these input test at the connector.
    • If any test indicates a problem, find and correct the cause, then recheck the system.
    • If all the input tests prove OK, replace the keyless receiver unit.
