Front Door Glass Outer Weatherstrip Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Starting at the front, pry the door glass outer weatherstrip (A) up to detach the clips (B), then slide the weatherstrip forward until the rear clip (C) touches the inside of the door panel.
  1. Twist the door glass outer weatherstrip (A) to pull the rear clip(B) out from the inside of the door, then remove the weatherstrip.
  1. Push the clip portions of the door glass outer weatherstrip into place securely.
