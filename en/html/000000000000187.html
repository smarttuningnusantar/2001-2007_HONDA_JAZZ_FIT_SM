Emblem/Sticker Replacement
NOTE: When removing the emblem/sticker, take care not to scratch the body.
  1. To remove the front ‘‘H'' emblem, remove the front grille.
  1. To remove the rear ‘‘H'' emblem, remove the rear license trim.
  1. Clean the body surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
  1. Apply the emblem/sticker, where shown.
  1. After installing the front ‘‘H'' emblem, reinstall the front grille to the front bumper, and reinstall the front bumper.
  1. After installing the rear ‘‘H'' emblem, reinstall the rear license trim.
