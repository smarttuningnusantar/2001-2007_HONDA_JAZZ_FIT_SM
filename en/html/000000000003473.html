Subfuel Pump Circuit Troubleshooting
If you suspect a problem with the subfuel pump, check that the subfuel pump actually runs; when it is on, you will hear some noise if you listen to the subfuel fill port with the subfuel fill cap removed. The subfuel pump should run for 2 seconds when the ignition switch is first turned on. If the subfuel pump does not make noise, check as follows:
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the subfuel pump relay (A).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the subfuel pump relay 5P connector terminals No. 1, No.5 and body ground individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the subfuel pump relay and the No. 7 SUBFUEL PUMP (15 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box. Also replace the No. 7 SUBFUEL PUMP (15 A) fuse.■
