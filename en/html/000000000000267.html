Sunroof Limit Switch Adjustment
  1. Remove the headliner.
Fully closed position adjustment
  1. With the sunroof wrench, close the glass (A) fully:
    • Make sure both link lifters (B) are parallel, and in the position shown.
    • Check the glass fit to the roof panel and the glass height.
  1. With an open-end wrench, loosen the limit switch mounting bolts (C).
  1. Adjust the limit switch (D):
    • Move the switch plate (E) a little at a time, then secure it at the position where you hear a faint click when the switch cam (F) pushes the position switch (open/close).
    • Check that the switch plate contacts the switch bracket (G).
One-stop position adjustment (for some models)
  1. With the sunroof wrench, operate the glass (A) to one-stop position.
  1. With an open-end wrench, loosen the position switch mounting bolts (C).
  1. Adjust the limit switch (D):
    • Move the switch plate (E) a little at a time, then secure it at the position where you hear a faint click when the switch cam (F) pushes the position switch (one-stop).
    • Check that the switch plate contacts the switch bracket (G).
  1. Check the operation of the glass by operating the sunroof switch:
    • With one-stop limit switch: From the tilt-up position to fully closed position, from the fully closed position to one-stop position, from the one-stop position to fully open position.
    • Without one-stop limit switch: From the tilt-up position to fully closed position, from the fully open position to the fully closed position.
  1. Check for water leaks. Let the water run fleely from a hose without a nozzle. Do not use a high-pressure spray.
