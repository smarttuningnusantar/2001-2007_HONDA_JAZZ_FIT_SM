Engine Oil Pan Removal
  1. Remove the heat cover.
  1. Remove the dipstick tube.
  1. Remove the clutch cover/torque converter cover (A), and remove the two bolts (B) securing the transmission.
  1. Remove the bolts/nuts securing the oil pan.
  1. Set the flat blade screwdriver as shown, and separate the oil pan from the block.
  1. Remove the oil pan.
