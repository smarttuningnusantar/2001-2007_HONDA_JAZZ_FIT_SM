Power Mirror Switch Test/Replacement
  1. Remove the driver's side dashboard side cover.
  1. Push the power mirror switch (A) out from the dashboard.
  1. Disconnect the 13P connector (B) from the switch.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the table.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the switch.
  1. Chech for continuity between the No. 9 and No. 4 terminals. If there is no continuty, replace the bulb (C).
