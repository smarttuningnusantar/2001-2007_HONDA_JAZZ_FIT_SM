Passenger's Dashboard Tray Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • LHD is shown, RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the dashboard.
  1. Remove the screws (A, B, C), pull the antenna lead (D) from bottom of the dashboard, then remove the passenger's instrument tray (E).
  1. Install the tray in the reverse order of removal.
