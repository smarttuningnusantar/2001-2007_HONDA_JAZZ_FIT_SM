Windshield Wiper Blade Setting Position
KQ model
  1. Pull up the wipers and activate them several times.
    NOTE: Please make sure to do this step in order to prevent the blades interference of driver's side and passenger's side.
  1. Return the wiper arm to the start position.
  1. Measure the distance from an edge of a cowl top to a wiper blade.
    Driver side:
    50 mm
    Passenger side:
    43 mm

  1. Remove the wiper arm and adjust the position if the measurements vary from the standard.
  1. Go back to Step 1 and 2 and confirm there is no interference of blades.
