Passenger's Dashboard Under Cover Removal/Installation
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  1. Remove the passenger's dashboard lower cover (A). LHD is shown, RHD is symmetrical.
    -1.
    Gently pull down the rear edge to release the clips.
    -2.
    Pull the cover away to release the pins (B) from the holders (C).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal.
