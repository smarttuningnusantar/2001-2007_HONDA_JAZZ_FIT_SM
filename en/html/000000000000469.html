CVT Drive Pulley Speed Sensor Replacement
  1. Disconnect the CVT drive pulley speed sensor connector.
  1. Remove the CVT drive pulley speed sensor.
  1. Install the new CVT drive pulley speed sensor (A) with the new O-ring (B). When installing the sensor, do not allow dust or other foreign particles to enter the transmission.
  1. Check the connector for rust, dirt, or oil, then connect the connector securely.
