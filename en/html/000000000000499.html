Rocker Arm Assembly Installation
  1. Install the rocker arm assembly (A) with dowel pin (B).
    NOTE: When reassembling the rocker arms, Install the rocker arm from cam chain side on the rocker shaft.
  1. Tighten each bolts two turns at a time in sequence below.

    Specified torque:

    29 N·m (3.0 kgf·m, 22 lbf·ft)
    Apply engine oil to the bolt threads and flange

  1. Adjust the valve clearance.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
