Initializing the OPDS (Occupant Position Detection System) Unit
Special Tools Required

When the seat-back cover, seat-back cushion, and/or OPDS unit are replaced, initialize the OPDS system by following the procedure below.
NOTE: Make sure the passenger's seat is dry. Set the seat-back in the normal position, and make sure there is nothing on the front passenger's seat.
  1. Make sure the ignition switch is OFF.
  1. Connect the DLC pin box (A) to the DLC (16P) (B), and connect the DLC pin box terminals No.4 and No.9 with a jumper wire (C), and push the operation switch.
  1. Connect the SCS short connector (D) to the MES connector (2P) (E). Do not use a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. The SRS indicator (F) comes on for about 6 seconds and goes off. Remove the SCS short connector from the MES connector within 4 seconds after the SRS indicator went off.
  1. The SRS indicator comes on again. Reconnect the SCS short connector to the MES connector within 4 seconds after the SRS indicator comes on.
  1. The SRS indicator goes off. Remove the SCS short connector from the MES connector within 4 seconds.
  1. Watch the SRS indicator.
    • If the indicator blinks two times and then stays on, the OPDS is initialized, but the DTCs need to be erased. Go to Step 9 , then erase the DTCs.
    • If the indicator blinks two times and then goes off, the OPDS unit is initialized. Go to Step 9 .
    • If the indicator stays on without first blinking, the OPDS is not initialized. Read the DTC, and go to the appropriate page in the DTC Troubleshooting Index.
