Tailgate Spoiler Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the body.
  • When prying component, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  1. Remove the tailgate spoiler (A):
    -1.
    Pry up at the knotch (B) of the lid (C), then remove the lids from both sides.
    -2.
    Remove the bolts.
    -3.
    Lift the spoiler up, then remove it.
  1. Install the spoiler in the reverse order of removal. When installing, three locating bolts (D) should be fully inserted into grommets on the tailgate.
