Intake Manifold Removal and Installation

Exploded View
Removal
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the brake booster vacuum hose (A) and positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) hose (B).
  1. Remove the engine wire harness holder (A) and water pipe (B).
  1. Remove the evaporative emission (EVAP) canister hose.

When Replacing the Intake Manifold:
  1. Remove the breather hose from the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the water bypass hoses.
  1. Remove the sub fuel feed hose guard.
  1. Remove the quick-connect fitting cover (A), then disconnect the sub fuel hose.
  1. Disconnect the sub fuel solenoid valve connector (2P).
  1. Remove the engine wire harness connectors and wire harness clamps from the intake manifold.
    • Throttle actuator connector
    • Manifold absolute pressure (MAP) sensor connector
    • Evaporative emission (EVAP) canister purge valve connector
  1. When replacing the intake manifold, remove the throttle body.
  1. Remove the intake manifold bracket A (A), then remove the intake manifold (B) and gasket (C).
Installation
  1. Install the intake manifold (A) with a new gasket (B), and tighten the bolts/nuts in a crisscross pattern in two or three steps, beginning with the inner bolt.
  1. Loosely install the intake manifold bracket A (C), and tighten the bolt (D) first, then the bolt (E).
  1. Install the throttle body (A) with a new gasket (B), if it was removed.
  1. Install the engine wire harness connectors and wire harness clamps from the intake manifold.
    • Throttle actuator connector
    • Manifold absolute pressure (MAP) sensor
    • Evaporative emission (EVAP) canister purge valve connector
  1. Connect the sub fuel solenoid valve connector (2P).
  1. Install the quick-connect fitting cover (A), then connect the sub fuel hose.
  1. Install the sub fuel feed hose guard.
  1. Install the water bypass hoses.
  1. Install the breather hose to the cylinder head cover.
  1. Install the evaporative emission (EVAP) canister hose.

If Intake Manifold was Replaced:
  1. Install the harness holder (A) and water pipe (B).
  1. Install the brake booster vacuum hose (A) and positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) hose (B).
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. After installation, check that all tubes, hoses and connectors are installed correctly.
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
