DTC Troubleshooting: 66
DTC 66:
 VSA Pressure Sensor (Softwear)
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II).
  1. Verify the DTC.
Is DTC 66 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. From the HDS Main Menu, select VSA, check VSA BRAKE PRESSER in the FUNCTION TEST.
Is the result?
YES -
Check for loose connector terminals in the VSA control unit 46P connector and repair if necessary. If terminals are OK, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
Check the brake lines for damage and leaks. Also check for bent brake lines. ■
