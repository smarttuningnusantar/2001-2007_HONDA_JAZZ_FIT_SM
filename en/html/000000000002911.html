Radiator and Fan Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the front bumper, and front bumper bracket.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose, lower radiator hose and Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) cooler hoses.
  1. Disconnect the fan motor connector, and remove the upper bracket and cushion, then pull up the radiator.
  1. Remove the fan shroud assembly and other parts from the radiator.
  1. Install the radiator in the reverse order of removal. Make sure the upper and lower cushions are set securely.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the front bumper bracket, and apply body paint to the mounting bolts, then install the front bumper.
  1. Fill the radiator with engine coolant and bleed the air.
