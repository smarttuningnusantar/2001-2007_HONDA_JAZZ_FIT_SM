Headlight Replacement
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) from the headlight (B).
    Headlight: 60/55 W
    Front Position Light: 5 W
    Front Turn Signal Light: 21 W

  1. Remove the three mounting bolts and the screw from the headlight.
  1. Remove the bolt and the corner upper beam (C).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After replacement, adjust the headlights to local requirements.
