How to Diagnose the Trouble Related to the Radio
How to diagnose the trouble when you can't receive any radio station, or at the extremely poor of the radio sensitivity
NOTE: Be the place where there is no obstacle in the neighborhood, and check it in the open air.
  1. Confirm the receiving conditions of the radio.
Do you receive each broadcasting station normally?
YES -
Radio operation is normal.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Confirm whether the antenna jack is inserted in the audio unit.
Is an antenna jack being inserted into the audio unit completely?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
An antenna jack is inserted in the audio unit completely again.■
  1. Check for continuity between the antenna jack and the roof antenna.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Replace the roof antenna.■
  1. Check for continuity between signal live centering signal line and the antenna jack outside of ground terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the roof antenna.■
NO -
Replace the audio unit.■
