Subfuel Tank Replacement
  1. Relieve subfuel pressure.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the right headlight.
  1. Remove the right cowl cover.
  1. Remove the right side front inner fender clips.
  1. Remove the clips securing the front fender fairing.
  1. Remove the fuel cap (A).
  1. Remove the right front fender.
  1. Remove the front fender fairing.
  1. Remove the wires (A) and washer hose (C).
  1. Remove the fuel subtank protector B.
  1. Disconnect the hoses (A), the subfuel pump 4P connector (B).
  1. Remove the subfuel tank (C).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
